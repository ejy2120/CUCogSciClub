Welcome to Columbia University's CogSci Club!

Welcome to the official website of CU CogSci Club, where we explore the fascinating intersections of cognitive science. Our club is dedicated to fostering a community of Columbia/Barnard students interested in understanding the mind and brain from various perspectives.

CU CogSci Club brings together students from diverse academic backgrounds who share a passion for cognitive science. Whether you're majoring in psychology, computer science, neuroscience, linguistics, or philosophy, you're welcome to join us!

Get Involved

Interested in joining CU CogSci Club? We'd love to have you on board! Stay updated on our upcoming events, discussions, and activities by becoming a member today. Reach out to us via email cucogsciclub@gmail.com or visit our next meeting to learn more.
